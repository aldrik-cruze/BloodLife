Make an Impact

Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is one of the most impactful things you can do for your community.

Save Multiple Lives

A single donation can be separated into components — red cells, plasma, platelets — saving up to 3 different patients.

Health Benefits

Regular donation reduces iron overload risk, stimulates blood cell production and may lower the risk of heart disease.

Build Community

Blood donation connects communities. Your generosity creates a network of care that everyone can rely on in emergencies.

Always Needed

Every 2 seconds someone needs blood. Hospitals always need fresh supply — there is no substitute for donated human blood.
